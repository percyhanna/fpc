Vision

Part of the original Foothills Philharmonic vision, the mandate of the Foothills Philharmonic Orchestra is to provide a place for all musicians to perform music spanning the Classical tradition, in small and medium ensembles, as well as full ensemble performances. Join us in bringing beautiful music to the Foothills!

Join Us!

Looking to join a community orchestra? We are looking for instrumentalists of all kinds and skill levels! Audition required.
