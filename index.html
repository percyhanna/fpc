About Us

The Foothills Philharmonic Society is committed to bring high quality choral and orchestral music to the southern Alberta Foothills region. We perform a variety of music, including the Orchestra and Chorus performing classical music from Palestrina, Bach, to Whitacre, or Jazz favourites with the Jazz Chorus.

Chorus Director: Tim Korthuis
Chorus Accompanist: Ana Loewen
Orchestra Director: Terry Lee
Jazz Chorus Director: Shawn Lindenbach
