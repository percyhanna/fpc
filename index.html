phil·har·mon·ic /ˌfilhärˈmänik /

From the Italian filarmonico which means ‘loving harmony’, the entire region of the Foothills could be described using this word. From choral groups, to instrumental ensembles, solo vocalists, jazz groups, and every other style, music is a vital part of the life of the Alberta Foothills.
