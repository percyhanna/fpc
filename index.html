About Us

The Foothills have a large number of fantastic musical groups, and we are bringing something new, while also old at the same time. Our goal is to bring beautiful choral music to the Foothills focusing on the more 'Classical' tradition, ranging from Palestrina and Bach, up to and including contemporary composers such as Allan Bevan and Eric Whitacre.

Conductor: Tim Korthuis
Accompanist: Ana Loewen
